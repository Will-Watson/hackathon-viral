The Yearrrrrr is 2428...

Mankind has lost all hope as what were once easily cured viruses have mutated to withstand their normal countermeasures. In a last ditch effort to thwart the inevitable disaster, The Convocation have agreed to Dr. Philip Wachowski's once laughed at procedure. What he calls, Truncation.

Dr. Wachowski's plan is to battle the viruses on the front lines. By this he means to literally fight the viruses face to face. To accomplish this, a select few volunteers will be chosen to participate in his "shrink ray" trials. Participants will be reduced to that of the size of a white blood cell and launched into his blood stream simulation where they will encounter enemies the likes of which have never been seen before.

As the viruses have grown immune to modern day weaponry, the volunteers will be armed with primitive 20th century weapons as, according to Dr. Wachowski "Of course it's going to work, they'll never see it coming!"

Are you ready to prove to Dr. Wachowski, The Convocation and the entire planet that you have what it takes to fight the viruses for real. Can YOU... make it to level 30.

Instructions

To play, all you need is your keyboard.

To move up, down, left and right you will use your arrow keys and to fire your gun you hold down the space bar. Most enemies will be vanquished with one shot, but beware, not all viruses are this weak.

Your clip holds twelve rounds. After expending your rounds you will be forced to reload. Not to worry though, as this is a simulation, you have unlimited clips.

Each level begins with a wave of enemies and you will not be able to progress until every last one of them are destroyed. You will have two seconds breathing room between levels. Good luck, and god speed.
